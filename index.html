How to Play

  • Press Start Puzzle to begin solving.
  • Drag and drop pieces into the grid to place them.
  • Double-click a piece to rotate it.
  • Press Undo to go one step back.
  • Press Reset Puzzle to restart the puzzle.
  • The Timer shows how much time you take.
  • Press Solve Puzzle to solve it using the Backtracking Algorithm (only after pressing Start Puzzle).
